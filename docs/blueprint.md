# **App Name**: Lagos Luxe

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero: Striking hero section with bold headline and lifestyle imagery to immediately convey the brand's modern African luxury.
- Shop Grid: Clean, minimalist product grid with high-quality images and subtle hover effects to enhance product discovery.
- PDP: Elegant product detail pages with large imagery, clear pricing, and a concise description. A prominent add-to-cart button should stand out.
- AI Outfit Visualizer: Generative AI tool: allow users to upload a photo and visualize themselves wearing different clothing items from the store's catalog. Provide size recommendations based on the image analysis.
- Brand Story: A captivating About Us section telling the brand's story with an emphasis on Lagos fashion culture, craftsmanship, and identity.
- Contact Form: A clean and simple contact form UI, subtly mentioning the Lagos location without detailed address specifics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep forest green (#228B22) to evoke a sense of luxury and connection to African heritage.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated off-white (#F5F5DC) provides a clean, premium backdrop, and lets the rich photography stand out.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#B8860B), used sparingly for highlights and CTAs, echoing the logo's accent color without overpowering the design.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for elegant, fashionable headlines. Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for readability in descriptions and body text.
- Luxury spacing and generous whitespace create a sense of exclusivity and allow the content to breathe, aligning with premium fashion branding.
- Subtle micro-interactions, smooth transitions, and loading animations enhance the user experience without being distracting. Aim for a fluid, polished feel.
